Chicken Parmesan


Ingredients:
  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for frying

Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C)./li>
  2. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper. Dip each in beaten eggs, then coat with a mixture of breadcrumbs and Parmesan cheese./li>
  3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Fry chicken until golden brown and cooked through, about 4 minutes per side./li>
  4. Place chicken in a baking dish. Top each piece with marinara sauce and shredded mozzarella cheese./li>
  5. Bake for 20 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly. Garnish with fresh basil before serving./li>
